About Me

I am Hiwot, an IT student and evening gym girl. I created this space to help girls grow strong without expensive dites or unrealistic pressure.

About Her Strong Era

Introduction

Hi, I’m Hiwot Kassa. I am an Information Technology student and fitness enthusiast dedicated to helping women build strength, confidence, and sustainable healthy habits.

Through Her Strong Era, I combine structured fitness guidance with realistic lifestyle strategies designed for university students, women of faith, and beginners restarting their journey.

Background & Journey

My fitness journey has not been perfect. I’ve taken breaks. I’ve struggled with consistency.

I’ve worried about my stomach area, my arms, and feeling confident in my own body.


After taking almost 3 months away from the gym, I decided to return — not just to lose weight, but to build discipline, confidence, and strength.



This time, I chose to do it smarter. With balance. With patience.
